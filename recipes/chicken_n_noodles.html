Chicken and Noodles

An Easy Chicken and Noodles Recipe

Description

Very easy chicken and noodles recipe. Requires few ingredients and is delicious.
Takes 30mins to make and serves 6.

Ingredients:

These are the ingredients you need:

  • 3 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (26 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cups diced, cooked chicken breast meat
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon seasoning salt
  • Â½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 (9 ounce) packages frozen egg noodles

Steps:

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Combine chicken broth, both condensed soups, and diced chicken in a large pot.
    Season with onion powder, seasoning salt, and garlic powder.
  3. Bring to a boil over high heat and stir in noodles.
  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer until desired consistency is reached, 20 to 30 minutes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
